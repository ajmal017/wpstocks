Registering

Click on the "Register" link on the top right.

Next, fill in the required fields. You will be required to enter a username, first name, last name, password and email address. The email address needs to be a real one.

Congratulations! You are now registered!

Administrative registration of users

Login to the backend of WP Stocks.

Click on Users -> Add New

Fill in the form and in the Role dropdown, select "WP Stocks"

Logging in (Splash page)

If you're not already on the login page click "Login" on the top right.

Next, enter your username and password.

If you have entered your usename and password correctly you will be taken to your control panel page. If not an alert will appear and you will be asked to try again.

Note: If you ever forget your password you can click on the "Forgot my password" link underneath the login form.

Control Panel

After you login you will be taken to your control panel. You will see a tab called "Dashboard". This tab will be selected.

You will see two sections: a client details section and an accounts section.

In the client details section you will see your name, company, address, telephone and fax number.

In the accounts section you will see a list of your accounts. Each account will show the account number, name and balance. Because you have to created any accounts, this section will be empty. Clicking on an account number will show details about that account

Creating an account

Click the "Add" button the the accounts section. You will be asked for a name for the account. Enter a name and click OK. A new account will be created and added to the accounts section.

Account statement

Each line in your account statement will show the following:

  • Date/Time - The date and time will be automatically recorded when you take the action with your account.
  • Symbol - It is abbreviated form of company’s equity share being purchased/sold by you with your account.
  • Quantity - It denotes the quantity of share purchased by you.
  • Price – Price per share being purchased by you.
  • Type – Whether it is deposit (DP), EB or FE(charges for found handling) or Withdraw(WD)
  • Reference - It provides short summary for each transaction being made by client including deposit, buying the share, Withdraw. ONE (1) % charge will be deducted in case of Buy and Withdraw cases.
  • Credit – What comes in is Credit i.e. it shows the amount which is being credited to account.
    • Debit – What goes out is Debit so it shows the amount which is being deducted from account for buying the share or fee deduction in case of Buying and Withdraw requests.
  • Balance - It shows the current balance of account.
  • Status - Finally the status which will be either ‘completed’ or ‘Pending’

Portfolio

Click on the Portfolio tab at the top of the page. Your portfolio will be displayed.

The portfolio table will have the following fields:

    Account
  • Symbol
  • Quantity
  • Price
  • Amount
  • Current Value
  • Profit

If you click on a symbol you will see a performance graph. You can adjust the graph to show daily, weekly, or monthly results.

Watch list

Underneath the portfolio table you will see a watch list table. This shows stocks you are following but haven't bought.

To add a stock to your watch list click on the "Add watch" button. You will be prompted for the stock symbol. Enter the stock symbol and click OK. After a few seconds the stock will be added to your watch list.

To remove a stock from your watch list click on the "Remove" button next to the stock.

Profile page

This page allows you to change your first name, last name, email and address.
